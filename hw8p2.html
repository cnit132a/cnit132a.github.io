Final Analytics Report Analysis

The GA4 Report provides me with a lot of important information about my website related to Views, Number of Users, Views per User, Average Engagement Time, and other useful aspects.

One thing that's very interesting and something I'm really interested in is the Pages and Screens panel that shows user behavior.

From February 18 to May 17, 2024, my website had 663 views, 50 users. I believe all users are classmates because I did not tell anyone outside the class to view my website.

According to Google Analytics, the highest number of views is for the Assignment page (288), 2nd is the Home page (127) and 3rd is the Contact page (58).

Some exercise pages like hw3, hw4 have more views than other pages and the average interaction time is up to 4m58s; however, the site hasn't any views of hw5 and hw7. The reason may be because I submitted those assignments on the last day of submission.

Google GA4 Analytics is a really useful tool. It helps me know how my website is performing and I can make appropriate adjustments to improve it, especially improving SEO traffic.
